Mountain Biking

During the pandemic, mountain biking emergered as a way to embrace the outdoors, get out of lockdown, all while safely social distancing. The benefits greatly make it fun with a bunch of productive side effects. The positives to starting out in mountain biking are:
  • Inexpensive to start
  • There's something for everyone at any skill level
  • You can go solo or in a group
  • The workout is full body(cardio, endurance, stamina)
  • Miles of trails near most urban areas
